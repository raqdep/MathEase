Our Mission

To develop an innovative educational technology platform as part of our Computer Science thesis project that makes General Mathematics accessible, enjoyable, and effective for Grade 11 STEM students. Through our research and development at Norzagaray College, we aim to contribute to the field of educational technology while helping students excel in mathematics.

Our Vision

To demonstrate the potential of educational technology through our Computer Science thesis project, contributing to the advancement of digital learning solutions in the Philippines. We envision MathEase as a stepping stone toward fostering confident and competent STEM students who are well-prepared for higher education and future careers in science and technology.
